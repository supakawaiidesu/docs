## Molten Network Ecosystem

The Molten Network hosts a growing ecosystem of decentralized applications (dapps) designed to leverage its high-speed, low-cost, and secure infrastructure. Currently, the primary product available on the network is the UniDex perp aggregator.

### UniDex Perp Aggregator

UniDex is a perpetual protocol deployed on the Molten Network using Molten Oracles to achieve instant updates and OEV protection. The protocol features a USDC pool that traders can use to open leveraged positions on various assets, including ETH, BTC, EUR/USD, and tokens like DOGE.

![UniDex](https://mintlify.s3-us-west-1.amazonaws.com/unidex/images/home-featured-image.png)

#### Key Features

- **Instant Updates:** Utilizes Molten Oracles for real-time price updates, ensuring accurate and up-to-date information for traders.
- **OEV Protection:** Benefits from Molten Network's oracle MEV protection, preventing frontrunning and other exploitative behaviors.
- **Diverse Trading Options:** Supports leveraged trading on a variety of assets, providing traders with multiple opportunities to engage in the market.
